Release of Retrofitted Harbourvale Line & Trains

5 mins read • By SMRTO

  • Retrofitting of new PSDs
  • New Trains

Good day, today we are releasing the 4 new Harbourvale Line Trains to the Files & Releases.

REMINDER: As part of this release, the support for older trains (the C651/A/B/C/D), will cease and not fit with the new HHPSDs/FHPSDs. If you intend to continue using those trains, do not use this release, and instead download the legacy version here.

Retrofitting of new PSDs

To fit the new trains, a full retrofitting was required to accomodate them. New features for those PSDs include a new lock/unlock sounds, to inject some realism.

New Trains

This release includes the 4 new trains - Alstom Movia R151, Kawasaki-CRRC Qingdao Sifang C151A/B/C.


Alstom Movia R151

For more details and to download this train, head over to this page.

Kawasaki-CRRC Qingdao Sifang C151C/B

For more details and to download these trains, head over to this page for C151C, or this page for C151B.

Kawasaki-CRRC Qingdao Sifang C151A

For more details and to download these trains, head over to this page for C151A.
