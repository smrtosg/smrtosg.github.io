Hillgrove Town

By Simulations by NRFZ  - March 25, 2024

  Project Description

Hillgrove Town 3.0 is a map made in-house by SVB Studios. This map includes 16 drivable services (2 will be introduced at a later date.)
Currently in BETA.

Changelogs (3.0):
- Major rationalization of Services - Introduction of new services 982, amendments of most services.
- New Adboards by Stellar Ace (selected Bus stops).
- Services 300 and 984 will be re-introduced at a later date.
- EDS formatting switched to SMRT's Mobitec format.
Note: Hillgrove Town and Woodlands Town's Fonts-Humans.7z & Vehicles.7z files are the same
